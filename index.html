Who I Am

About

Curious about how money moves.

I work in asset operations at BetterInvest, a Chennai-based fintech platform that connects investors with opportunities in media and entertainment through invoice discounting and revenue-based financing.

Most of my time goes into the nuts and bolts — making sure disbursements happen on time, receivables are tracked properly, and investors get clear visibility into their assets. It's not glamorous work, but it's the kind that keeps platforms running.

I studied Commerce with Banking & Insurance at Sri Krishna Arts and Science College. Before BetterInvest, I spent time at Flipkart and JSW Steel in different capacities, which gave me a feel for how operations work across very different industries.

I write here to think through ideas — about fintech, alternative investments, and the systems that make finance work. Outside of work, I make music, play piano, and occasionally overthink things.
